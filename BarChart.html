Animated Bar chart

In this chart the global land average temperature data has been split into months of their respective years, because the data from 1750 till 1900 had a very high uncertainty factor i have decided to leave those years out.

On the left we can see the data of the year 1900, this will be visable throughout the animation to show the increase and decrease of the temperature as comparison.

Click on start to start the animation, click on stop to stop the animation.

It is also possible to add years manually but when doing this stop the animation of wait for it to finish please.
